Week 10 : Project Update (Updated : 25/3/2023)

5 Questions

What is your project about?
US version of Survivor Reality TV competition

What is the data you plan to use?
The show's ratings / viewership, demographics LINK

What is the question you plan to answer?
Why should I watch the show and where should I start watching

Why is this an important question?
Survivor is one of the longest running reality TV show that have lasted for 23 years (and counting).
Survivor's ratings have always been high on average but the viewership is on a decline, so it is important to introduce new viewers to the show.
Survivor does a good job at representation through a diverse cast so it carries a lot of important stories and narrativies that need to be seen.

Which rows and columns of the dataset do you plan to use, to answer this question?
I am using multiple data files but for viewers.csv, I am using labels of Seasons, Average imdb_rating and viewers number. Since the data includes Australian and South African version of the show, I will be filtering those data out.
In terms of the castaways.csv, I will be using the order of their elimination and then will be filtering out the Australian and South African version of the show.


Changes to Datastory Flow / Concept

The focus and flow of the datastory of Week 9 felt a bit all over the place so I have decided to narrow the datastroy down for mainly new viewers or people curious about survivor. The new flow of the datastory would be

1. Introduction to what Survivor is
2. Brief introduction about where it have been filmed and where the contestants are mostly from - maps
3. Viewership & Rating -> which season to watch
4. Why you should watch -> Demographic & Representation


Changes / Update Summary

1. Overall change of sequence of the datastory, with omission of some deeper gameplay elements to focus more on the new viewers.

2. Added episodes rating and seasons to watch chart
